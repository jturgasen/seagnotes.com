Software Engineering at Google: Lessons Learned from Programming Over Time

Software Engineering at Google: Lessons Learned from Programming Over Time is a collection of time-won software engineering knowledge that was released in March of 2020. The book is a body of insights from millions of person-hours at Google; a true gem. Thanks to everyone at Google from the guy who fixed a ' to whoever sacrificed the most sleep to get it done - thank you.

In the current era of human progress computer software is truly the foundation for the next steps and leaps in large scale human advancement. Software is a great leveler and every coder can contribute to the better software engineering endeavor. I hope my notes, and more so Google’s book, can help you help us all move forward in some small way. -- JoshT
